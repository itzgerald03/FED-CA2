GOALS

Saving The Earth

Saving the Earth demands a shift to renewable energy, adoption of sustainable practices, conscious consumer choices, and increased public awareness. Together, these actions will safeguard the environment and secure a sustainable future for our planet.

Personal Goals

My first goal is to practice the 3Rs (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle) in my daily life. I aim to minimize single-use items such as plastic bags and containers, and instead use reusable recycle bags when grocery shopping and bringing my own reusable container when buying take-away. By doing so, this reduces waste which helps the environment.

My second goal is to reduce my carbon footprint. I can do so by taking public transportation, or walking instead of taking taxis. I will also turn off lights, electronics, and appliances when not in use. Encourage energy-efficient practices at home and at school to reduce electricity consumption.

Lastly, I plan to conserve water, I will remember to turn off the tap when not used and take shorter showers. I will also try to utilize rainwater for gardening and cleaning, and wait for full laundry loads before running the washing machine. I will also try to promote water-saving practices among my families and friends.
